Startup Bookstore

Published by Filippo Diotalevi on 13th November 2010 (last update 13th November 2010)

A few people have asked me recommendations for good resources/books on lean startups. That's my list:

  • Four steps to the epiphany let me say it clearly: this book is boring, badly structured and poorly written. However, it all started here. The Customer Development chapter, in particular, contains the essence of lean startups
  • Startup Lessons Learned this book contains all the archive of Eric Ries' blog. A lot of practical experience in applying lean and agile methods in startups
  • The Entrepreneurs Guide to Customer Development for Tech Startups a sort of cheat sheet to "Four steps to the epiphany". Contains a lot of case studies and summaries the lean startup thinking
  • Startup Marketing,Unlocking Startup Growth honestly, don't trust any marketeer who doesn't know the work of Sean Ellis
  • The Venture Hacks Bible not directly related to lean startup, but that's a 1100+ page pdf containing everything you need to know
  • Pitching Hacks the art of pitching explained
